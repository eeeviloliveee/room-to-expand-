What people say

Experiences shared by people who have had sessions with Sean.

My Pantarei sessions with Sean have been a soft and supportive space for connecting with my emotions on a deeper level. Creating space to really feel what is happening in my body has been very meaningful for me. When I don't fully understand what I'm feeling or find myself stuck, I usually turn to non-verbal practices like dance, breathwork, or movement. Working with Sean offered something different, a gentle way to begin putting feelings I couldn't fully grasp into words. Through his thoughtful questions, what felt unclear started to take shape and expressing them was really relieving and liberating. I'm grateful for the way he creates a space of empathy and non-judgment, allowing a genuine sense of exploration to unfold. It felt like a space where I can truly be myself, where crying, laughing, and all feelings were welcome.
